# Run YumCut on Your Phone - 3 Steps

## Step 1 - Open Codespace
Tap this link on your phone:

https://codespaces.new/mudassaraiai-collab/yumcut-claude-video

Sign in with GitHub if asked. Wait 2 minutes for setup to complete automatically.

## Step 2 - Add your API key
When Codespace opens, the .env file will open automatically.

Find this line:
  ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=paste_your_key_here

Replace paste_your_key_here with your real key from:
  https://console.anthropic.com/settings/keys

Save the file (tap the save icon or Ctrl+S).

## Step 3 - Open the app
The app starts automatically. Look for a popup saying "Open in Browser" and tap it.

Or tap the Ports tab at the bottom and tap the globe icon next to port 3000.

## Your pages
- /lego  - Kids Lego Video Maker

- /       - YumCut home

## That is it!
Everything installs automatically. No command line needed.
